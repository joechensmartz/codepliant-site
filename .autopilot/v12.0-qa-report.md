- Billing platform flagged for COPPA compliance -- seems incorrect

## Verdict

**v12.0 is a strong release.** The framework-implicit detection for Django, Rails, and Laravel closes the biggest gap from v11.0. Overall accuracy improved from 72% to 83%. The weakest ecosystems all saw 25-30 point accuracy improvements. Monorepo detection works well (Cal.com: 113 workspaces, 26 services).

**Remaining work is catalog expansion** (more Java/Go libraries) rather than architectural gaps.
