## 7. Escalation Procedures

### 7.1 Inbound Communication

1. **Receipt:** All inbound regulatory communications must be forwarded to the DPO within 4 hours
2. **Logging:** DPO logs the communication in Section 1 within 24 hours
3. **Triage:** DPO assesses urgency and assigns priority
4. **Escalation:** High-priority matters escalated to Legal Counsel and Executive Sponsor within 24 hours
5. **Response:** Draft response within the regulatory deadline (default: 30 days unless otherwise specified)
6. **Review:** Legal Counsel reviews all outbound responses before sending
7. **Follow-up:** DPO tracks any required follow-up actions in Section 6

### 7.2 Proactive Notifications

1. **Assessment:** DPO determines notification obligation (breach, DPIA consultation, etc.)
2. **Drafting:** DPO prepares notification using approved templates
3. **Approval:** Legal Counsel and Executive Sponsor approve
4. **Submission:** DPO submits via authority's official channel
5. **Logging:** Record in Sections 1 and 4 (if breach-related)
