## 8. Assessment Workflow

```
1. Business owner submits vendor request
     ↓
2. DPO sends this questionnaire to vendor
     ↓
3. Vendor completes and returns with evidence
     ↓
4. DPO scores responses and classifies risk
     ↓
5a. Low/Medium Risk → DPO approves (with conditions if medium)
5b. High Risk → Privacy Steering Committee review
5c. Critical Risk → Rejected
     ↓
6. DPA executed, vendor added to sub-processor list
     ↓
7. Annual reassessment scheduled
```
