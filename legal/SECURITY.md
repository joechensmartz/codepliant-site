## Payment & PCI Considerations

This project integrates with payment processing services. We follow PCI DSS guidelines for handling payment data:

- Payment card data is never stored on our servers; it is handled by our PCI-compliant payment processor
- Vulnerabilities related to payment flows, billing data exposure, or payment bypass are treated as **critical**
- If you discover a payment-related vulnerability, please include `[PCI]` in your report subject line

## AI Safety & Security

This project integrates with AI/ML services. In addition to traditional security concerns, we are interested in reports related to:

- Prompt injection vulnerabilities
- Data leakage through AI model interactions
- Unauthorized access to AI-generated content
- Model manipulation or adversarial inputs
- AI output used to bypass security controls

We follow a coordinated disclosure process. We ask that you:
